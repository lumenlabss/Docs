Introduction

LumenOne is the open-source website management panel developed with Node.js. It lets you easily manage your projects thanks to a modern, intuitive and high-performance interface, designed for developers and hosting providers.

Why LumenOne?

Our mission is to provide a free, powerful alternative to proprietary hosting panels. LumenOne combines simplicity with professional features to give you full control over your web infrastructure.

  • Modern Tech Stack (Node.js)
  • Intuitive User Interface
  • Extensible & Open Source
